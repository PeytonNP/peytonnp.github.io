Applied and selected as a participant for the Chicago Datathon where undergraduate and graduate students from the Midwest came together to analyze various datasets. As teams, we were asked to pose a question and answer it within given time and resource constraints.

ASA Datafest Purdue University

April 2017

The American Statistical Association Datafest celebrates data and brings together the Data Science community by hosting a competition where teams of undergraduate students spend a weekend analyzing real datasets and preparing a presentation for judges and other industry professionals.

Rose-Hulman Undergraduate Mathematics Conference

April 2017

Made and delivered a brief presentation about the work I completed during the academic year as a student researcher. The conference invites students and professionals to give talks based on their work in primarily STEM fields and hosts various workshops for the attendees.

Qualcomm DECA Conference

January 2017

In December of 2016, I was selected to attend the Qualcomm Diversity Engineering Campus Alignment Conference where Qualcomm provides professional development and other resources to minority and underrepresented students who study STEM by bringing them together to their San Diego headquarters for a weekend full of activities, workshops, and events.

Grace Hopper ConferenceCelebration of Women in Computing

October 2016

Selected by Purdue University and provided with travel and housing funds to attend the annual Grace Hopper Conference. The Grace Hopper Celebration of Women in Computing is the world's largest gathering of women technologists. It is produced by the Anita Borg Institute and presented in partnership with ACM.

ACM Richard Tapia Conference Celebration of Diversity in Computing

September 2016

Applied and selected by Purdue University to attend the annual ACM Richard Tapia Conference which brings together individuals of all backgrounds to acknowledge the advancements made in computing while recognizing minorities and individuals with disabilities that have made significant contributions to the field of computing.
