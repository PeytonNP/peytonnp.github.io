Hi! My name is Peyton Puckett and I recently graduated from Purdue University with a B.S. in Computer Science with a Concentration in Machine Intelligence. I will begin working for AT&T this summer within their Technology Development Program as a Software Engineering. During my final semester in college, I was able to participate in a research course focused on Big Spatial Data Systems. More information about the work can be found here. I also worked as a Student Developer for the Purdue Math Department working to create a tool for Professors and Course Coordinators. Over the past few years, I've completed two summer internships at AIG, one in Charlotte, NC and the other in Houston, TX. I also had the opprotunity to work on a research project in Computational Chemistry with a focus on Big Data Analysis during my sophomore year at Purdue thanks to a program call Statistics Living Learning Community. In my free time, I enjoy keeping up with the lastest consumer technology news, acoustic guitar, biking, and photography. In the next five years, I hope to work in technology and develop healthcare applications in my free time. Feel free to browse my site to learn more and reach out to me for anything!
